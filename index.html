What is E.A.T schools

EATSCHOOLS is an interactive educational website offering a comprehensive learning experience. It features a vast question bank, each with an accompanying explanatory video. Video lectures and simulations further enhance understanding and engagement. Students are rewarded with EATCOINs for completing quizzes, which can be redeemed for additional resources, tutoring sessions, or physical rewards. With its user-friendly interface and innovative rewards, EATSCHOOLS is becoming a top choice for students seeking academic improvement. It provides the necessary tools and resources for success in exams and expanding knowledge.

E.A.T schools system

  • Center-based System

    There will be one session per week for explanation and problem-solving, with a duration of 3 hours. Additionally, there will be exams and assignments on our website after each session.

  • Online System

    We provide the content to you through our website, eatschools.com.

  • Online + Center System

    In this system, you will be participating in the online system, and you will have the option to attend the in-person session at the center.
